Captain Jenni

Captain Jenni can be summed up by saying that she is tenacious, knowledgeable and a lover of the sea. She has a passion for exploring our waters, nature and the wildlife that call our area their habitat. Jenni grew up on Captiva Island and is on the Sanibel vegetation committee protecting local plants, helps to work with sea turtle nesting season and is involved with assisting injured animals get to the C.R.O.W. wildlife rehabilitation center. She also participates in catch and release fishing tournaments to help our fishery gather more data to better manage our ecosystem. Captain Jenni also recently became a Master Naturalist.

Tenacious. Knowledgeable. Passionate.

Very few licensed captains have the long term local knowledge to provide such an amazing experience beyond compare. Her kind demeanor and perceptive insight also lends itself to a vast knowledge of history from the present to the Calusa natives that inhabited these islands for many centuries before. She loves to share her expertise with her charter guests and is always a wealth of information. Every trip leaves the boat with knowledge learned and a greater appreciation for the special place that she calls home. Enjoying it all with other adventurous people is what she loves to do!

Andros 26' Tarpon

This boat has been a labor of love by her owner, Captain Jenni. The hull had a complete factory rebuild in 2022 and was re-powered with a new motor in 2021. It allows access to shallow areas that many other boats simply can't reach. This provides excellent opportunities to see our pristine grass and sand flats where wildlife and sea life is abundant. It's Hybrid design also allows access to the barrier island beaches with a smooth and dry ride. With a large motor she can get places quickly when conditions permit and with her sharp bow entry can slow down in rougher conditions to provide a better experience for our guests. The boat has padded forward seating and a padded cooler seat for your comfort aboard. It also provides plenty of dry storage and a large cooler that will be iced down before your trip for all of the food and beverages you would like to bring.

  • 300 hp Suzuki
  • LOA: 26' 2"
  • Beam: 8' 2"
  • Draft: 10"
  • Fuel Capacity: 80 gallons
  • Porta Bracket system for shallow water operations
  • Dual Power Poles for shallow water anchoring
  • Second upper station provides excellent wildlife spotting
  • US Coast Guard approved safety equipment for everyone aboard
